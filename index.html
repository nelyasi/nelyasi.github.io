Seyed Navid Elyasi

PhD Researcher  ·  Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg, Sweden

I began programming at an early age working with microcontrollers in C/C++ and building embedded systems such as line-following robots. Over time my interests shifted toward scientific computing and quantum technologies.

During my university studies I used Python extensively for computational physics and numerical simulations. This evolved into a specialisation in quantum technologies during my MSc, working with Qiskit, QuTiP, and Strawberry Fields.

My current research focuses on Quantum Data Centers and Distributed Quantum Computing, with broader interests in open quantum systems, high-performance computing, and machine learning.
